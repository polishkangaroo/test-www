About me

I split my time between zoology and science communication.
My interests encompass behavioural ecology and wildlife conservation, as well as broader issues, such as women in science or internationalisation of science.
I work in English and Polish.

Academia

An Oxford-educated zoologist working as Senior Lecturer in Environmental Sciences at Brunel University London.
My research background is in ecology; I have diverse teaching experience, from curriculum design and strategic planning to running lectures and field courses.

Science communication

Winner of FameLab Poland and I’m a Scientist, get me out of here! Presenter for the Discovery Channel.
I currently work as a Communications and Public Engagement Officer at the University of Oxford, as well as a freelance science writer, speaker and coach.
